About Me: The Visionary Behind PQ Studios

I am Sai Kumar Maddina, the founder and principal photographer at PQ Studios Hyderabad. With over eight years of dedicated expertise in professional photography, I have established our studio as Hyderabad's premier destination for exceptional visual storytelling.

My journey in photography began with formal education in visual arts, complemented by continuous mastery of evolving photographic techniques. This academic foundation, combined with extensive field experience, enables me to approach each project with both artistic vision and technical precision.

My philosophy is encapsulated in this guiding principle: "Great photography transcends mere visual representation; it evokes emotion, tells stories, and creates lasting impact." This ethos informs every project we undertake, from intimate portrait sessions to comprehensive commercial productions.
