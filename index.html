Our Story: Where Vision Meets Expertise

Welcome to PQ Studios, the premier photography studio in Hyderabad founded and led by Sai Kumar Maddina. For over eight years, we have stood at the forefront of visual storytelling, crafting images that resonate with authenticity, emotion, and unparalleled artistry.

Founded on the principle that every moment deserves to be captured with intention and excellence, PQ Studios has evolved from a passionate venture into Hyderabad's trusted name for professional photography. Our journey reflects our commitment to transforming ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.

The Visionary Behind the Lens: Sai Kumar Maddina

As the creative force and founder of PQ Studios, Sai Kumar Maddina brings a rare combination of formal education in visual arts and over eight years of hands-on expertise. Holding advanced qualifications in photography, Sai approaches each project not merely as an assignment, but as a collaborative artistic journey.

His philosophy is simple yet profound: "Great photography isn't about what you see, but what you make others feel." This ethos permeates every project, from intimate portrait sessions to large-scale commercial productions.
